About

Hey, my name is Gabriel Silk, and I've been writing software for about 20 years. I entered college when I was 14, studied computer science / math, and landed my first job at Electronic Arts when I was 17. For the last six years I've worked professionally in Silicon Valley, first for Quantcast and then as a founding member at Nomic, where I explored some product ideas in the recruiting space with my colleagues Nate Boyd and Aaron Boyd. Now I'm a consultant, helping companies of all stages/sizes with their technical and product needs.

My product philosophy is functional minimalism: that is to say, function before flashiness, without loss of comprehensibility. As far as coding, I believe that every line is a liability, the best diff is all minuses, and that if your code outweighs your tests, you're in trouble.

In my spare time I like to climb, travel, read books, make weird music, and work on open source software.

If you're interested in getting in touch, email me at: gabesilk@gmail.com
